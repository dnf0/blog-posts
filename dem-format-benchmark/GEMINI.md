# Workspace Instructions

## Blog Post Style Guidelines
When writing or editing blog posts in this repository, you MUST strictly adhere to the guidelines established in the [`BLOG_STYLE.md`](../BLOG_STYLE.md) document. 

This style guide enforces a rigorous, academic tone using British English, prevents the use of hyperbolic marketing buzzwords, and outlines requirements for empirical anchoring, avoiding single-sentence paragraphs, explaining domain jargon, and framing architectural decisions around practical cloud deployment realities and organizational trade-offs.
